Every quarter we meet founders who can recite their MRR to the dollar but can't answer basic questions about gross margin or burn multiple. Growth without clarity is a slow-motion crisis.

## The three numbers every founder should know cold

- **Cash runway in months** — not just cash on hand
- **Contribution margin per customer** — revenue minus variable cost
- **CAC payback period** — how long until a customer pays back their acquisition cost

## Clarity compounds

Clear numbers make hiring decisions easier. They make investor conversations shorter. They reduce the time spent re-litigating decisions that should have been settled by data.

If your monthly close takes more than ten business days, that's the first thing to fix.
