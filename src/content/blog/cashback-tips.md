Cashback programs look simple on the surface, but the gap between an average user and an optimized one can be 3–4x. Here are five strategies we recommend to clients.

## 1. Match cards to your actual spend mix

Pull a 90-day expense report and group spend by merchant category. Pick the card whose top earning categories overlap with your top three categories.

## 2. Stack a flat-rate card for everything else

A 2% flat-rate card catches the long tail of purchases that don't fit any bonus category.

## 3. Pay statement balances early

Some cards reduce rewards if utilization stays high. Paying mid-cycle keeps utilization low without sacrificing earnings.

## 4. Watch for rotating bonus quarters

Set a calendar reminder when quarterly categories change — most cardholders forget to enroll.

## 5. Reconcile rewards monthly

Treat cashback as revenue. If it isn't reconciled, it isn't real.
