"Sustainable business" gets used loosely. We use it in the literal sense: a business whose unit economics, cash position, and operating cadence let it survive a 12-month revenue contraction.

## Three tests for sustainability

1. **The 12-month test** — could you survive flat revenue for a year without layoffs?
2. **The customer concentration test** — would losing your top customer break you?
3. **The vendor lock-in test** — how quickly could you replace your most critical vendor?

If any answer makes you uncomfortable, that's where to invest planning time this quarter.
