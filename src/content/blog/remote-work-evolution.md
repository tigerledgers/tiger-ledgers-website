Remote work changed finance more than most functions. The internal controls built around an in-office close — physical document review, in-person approvals, shared printers for invoices — all needed to be re-engineered.

## What works in distributed finance

- **Async-first close calendar** with explicit owner per task
- **Document-of-record discipline** — every artifact lives in one system
- **Time-boxed standups** twice a week, not daily

## What doesn't

Trying to recreate the in-office experience over Zoom. The goal isn't to replicate the old way; it's to design a better way that happens to be remote-friendly.
